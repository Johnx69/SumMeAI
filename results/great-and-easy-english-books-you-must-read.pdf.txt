 Reading English novels is a great way to improve language skills, as it helps with vocabulary, understanding, and knowledge of different cultures. Charlotte's Web by E.B. White and Mieko and the Fifth Treasure by Eleanor Coerr are recommended books for EFL learners. The House On Mango Street by Sandra Cisneros follows the story of Esperanza, a Mexican girl living in a new home in a busy Latino area of Chicago. The Giver by Lois Lowry is about an old fisherman, Santiago, and the fight he has with the biggest fish he ever caught. Number the Stars by Lois Lowry is a realistic novel based on World War II history. A Wrinkle In Time by Madeline L'engle follows the story of Meg, a 14-year-old troublemaker who embarks on a journey to save the universe. Reading is a great way to improve English and learn new vocabulary.
