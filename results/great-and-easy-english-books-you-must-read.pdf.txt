 Reading English novels is a great way to improve language skills and build vocabulary. Charlotte's Web by E.B. White and Mieko and the Fifth Treasure by Eleanor Coerr are recommended for EFL learners. The House On Mango Street by Sandra Cisneros follows Esperanza, a Mexican girl living in a busy Latino area of Chicago. The Giver by Lois Lowry is about a young boy who is given the special job of Receiver of Memories. Number the Stars by Lois Lowry is a realistic novel based on World War II history. A Wrinkle In Time by Madeline L'engle follows Meg, a 14-year-old troublemaker, as she uses a tesseract to travel through the universe and find her father. Reading is a great way to learn English without stress.
