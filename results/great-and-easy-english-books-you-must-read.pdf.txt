 Reading English novels is a great way to improve language skills, as it helps with vocabulary, understanding, and knowledge of different cultures. It also allows you to move at your own pace. Popular books for English learners include Charlotte's Web by E.B. White, Mieko and the Fifth Treasure by Eleanor Coerr, The House On Mango Street by Sandra Cisneros, The Giver by Lois Lowry, Peter Pan by J.M. Barrie, and A Wrinkle In Time by Madeline L'engle. These books are easy to understand and provide a deep understanding of different cultures. Reading is a great way to learn English without stress and can be fun and educational.
